Account Deletion Request

To delete your account and associated data in Ounce App:

  1. Open the app and go to Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm deletion. This action is irreversible.

Data Handling

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently erased:

  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • App usage history

Note: Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for legal compliance.
