Account Deletion Request

To delete your account and associated data in Ounce App, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Ounce App on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab
  3. Tap on Delete Account button
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Handling Policy

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your personal account information (username, email, profile details)
  • All app usage data and preferences
  • Any stored content you created in the app

Note: Some anonymized statistical data may be retained for analytical purposes, but this data cannot be linked back to your identity. All personally identifiable information will be permanently deleted immediately.
